Medicine Overview Warnings

  USES

It is used for Acute painful musculoskeletal conditions, periods pain, joint pain,flu, cold,toothache, headache, ear pain, fever.

  SIDE EFFECTS

Side Effects includes: Dry mouth, tachycardia, palpitation, urinary retention,
blurred vision, weakness, nausea, vomiting,headache

  When Not To Use

Hypersensitivity to the active ingredient, Hepatic impairment, glaucoma, myasthenia gravis, prostatic hypertrophy, pyloric or duodenal obstruction, stenosing peptic ulcers

  Precaution

Do not drive or operate machinery

  Precaution

Donot take on an empty stomach. Donot exceed the recommended dose.

  Precaution

Carefully measure the dose using a special measuring device/spoon.

  Precaution

Avoid using it if allergic to paracetamol.Tell your doctor if your condition persists or worsens.

  Pregnancy

Please consult your doctor when taking this medicine if you are pregnant

  Drugs to Avoid

Drugs that may interact with this drug include Ketoconazole, Leflunomide, Prilocaine, Teriflunomide.
