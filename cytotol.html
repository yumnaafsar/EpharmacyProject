Medicine Overview Warnings

  USES

This medication is used in combination with another drug (mifepristone) to end a pregnancy (abortion).

  SIDE EFFECTS

It can cause severe ongoing stomach discomfort or diarrhea, feeling very thirsty or hot, being unable to urinate, heavy sweating, or hot and dry skin.

  When Not To Use

Hypersensitivity to misoprostol, prostaglandins, or prostaglandin analogues.

  Precaution

May cause diarrhea, should not be coadministered with other drugs that cause diarrhea (eg, magnesium-containing antacids)

  Precaution

Before using this medication, tell your doctor or pharmacist your medical history, especially of: stomach/intestinal disease (e.g., inflammatory bowel disease), risk factors for uterine rupture when this drug is used vaginally (e.g., prior Cesarean delivery, uterine surgery, five or more previous pregnancies).

  Precaution

Safety and efficacy in patients with cardiovascular disease, diabetes, renal impairment, or respiratory disease are not established.

  Precaution

Use with caution in women >35 years or heavy smokers (these patients were not included in clinical trials).

  Pregnancy

This drug is known to cause issues during pregnancy, therefore please do not take this drug if you are pregnant.

  Drugs to Avoid

Some interactions with this drug include Crofelemer, eluxadoline
