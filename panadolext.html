Medicine Overview Warnings

  USES

Some days you need a more powerful pain reliever. The combination of ingredients in Panadol Extra provides up to 37% more powerful pain relief than standard paracetamol. 1 - 2 tablets every 4 to 6 hours as required (Maximum 8 tablets in 24 hours)he ingredients in Panadol Extra treats Migraine pain, Tension type headache Back Ache and Tooth ache.

  SIDE EFFECTS

Do not take more frequently than every 4 hours because it can cause dizziness. Do not give to children under 12 years.

  When Not To Use

It Contains paracetamol so do not use with any other paracetamol-containing products. The concomitant use with other products containing paracetamol may lead to an overdose.

  Precaution

It contains caffeine. Avoid excessive intake of caffeine containing drinks (eg. tea, coffee etc.) when taking this.

  Precaution

Paracetamol overdose may cause liver failure which can lead to liver transplant or death. Patients who have been diagnosed with liver or kidney impairment must seek medical advice before taking this medication.

  Precaution

Do not take more than the recommended dose.

  Precaution

If symptoms persist, medical advice must be sought. Keep out of sight and reach of children

  Pregnancy

Paracetamol-caffeine is not recommended for use during pregnancy due to the possible increased risk of spontaneous abortion associated with caffeine consumption.

  Not Recommended for

This product is contraindicated in patients with a previous history of hypersensitivity to paracetamol, caffeine or excipients.
